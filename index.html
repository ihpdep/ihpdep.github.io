About Me

I am a 3rd year M.Sc student in Internet Vision Group at Zhejiang University under supervision of Prof. Jianke Zhu. Before that, I received my Bachelor degree in Software Engineering from East China Normal University . Currently, I am looking for a Ph.D. position.

Research Interest

My research interest are in the areas of Computer Vision and Machine Learning. I believe that these fields are the directions leading to true Artificial intelligence which can help human being step into a better world. Currently, I am very interested in how to find links between frames which lies at the intersection among many topics, from low-level like optical flow, video segmentation, object tracking and to high level scene understanding.
