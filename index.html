About me

I am a 3th year M.Sc student supervised by Prof. Jianke Zhu in Internet Vision Group. Before that, I received my Bachelor degree in Software Engineering from East China Normal University .

Research interest

I am interested in Computer Vision and Machine Learning. I believe that these fields are the directions leading to true Artificial Intellegence which can help human being step into a better world. Currently, I am very interested in how to find links between frames which lies at the intersection among many topics, from low-level to high level, like optical flow, video segmentation, object tracking and scene understanding.
