About Me

I am a 4th year Ph.D. candidate in Smart Vision Lab at Zhejiang University under supervision of Prof. Jianke Zhu. Before that, I received my Bachelor degree in Software Engineering from East China Normal University and Master degree in College of Computer Science from Zhejiang University in 2016. In 2018, I was visiting Prof. Michael Yip's Advanced Robotics and Controls Lab (ARClab) at University of California, San Diego.

Research Interest

My research interests are in the areas of Computer Vision and Machine Learning and, particularly, in methods of extracting meaningful information and structural data from videos and raw streaming sources. I am very interested in how to find links between frames which lies at the intersection among many topics, from low-level like optical flow, video segmentation, object tracking and to high level scene understanding. Currently, my focus is on model-free single object tracking and how to apply vision methods into robotic area.
